About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an exhilarating survival action game where you face endless waves of monsters in a constantly evolving world. Choose from unique characters, each with their own special abilities, and battle to survive as long as possible against increasingly difficult enemies.

As you progress, collect experience points to level up and unlock new powerful skills. Discover and equip various weapons and items to enhance your combat abilities and survival chances. With procedurally generated levels, every playthrough offers a fresh and challenging experience.

Can you master the art of survival and become the ultimate Monster Survivor?

Game Controls

Keyboard

  • Movement: WASD or Arrow Keys
  • Special Ability: Space
  • Pause: ESC
  • Inventory: I
  • Character Stats: C

Mobile

  • Movement: Virtual Joystick
  • Special Ability: Ability Button
  • Pause: Menu Button
  • Inventory: Inventory Icon
  • Character Stats: Character Icon
